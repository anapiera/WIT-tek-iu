About TEK

Technology • Business • Innovation

Who We Are

Tau Epsilon Kappa (TEK) at Indiana University is a selective, co-ed professional technology fraternity designed for students who want to go beyond the classroom.

What We Do

We host technical workshops, industry panels, and real-world projects that prepare our members for top careers in tech and business.

Our Community

TEK is a network of ambitious students who collaborate, support one another, and build lifelong professional connections.
