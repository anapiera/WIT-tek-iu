From One Idea to a Growing Legacy

Tau Epsilon Kappa was founded at the University of Michigan – Ann Arbor by Trisha Kant in Spring 2022, creating a space where students passionate about technology and growth could build something meaningful together.

Today, TEK’s alumni network continues to play a vital role within the organization. Alumni serve as career advocates, mentors, and close friends — remaining deeply connected to the community they helped build.

The expansion to Indiana University began in Fall 2025, when early conversations sparked the vision of bringing TEK’s mission to a new campus.

Founded by Neeharika Nair and Alekhya Vadiala, the IU chapter was officially established in Spring 2026 as the Beta Chapter — bringing together a new generation of driven and collaborative students.

Building the Future at IU

With its Alpha Class, TEK IU is establishing the foundation for a lasting legacy — one focused on innovation, growth, and a community that extends far beyond college.
