Who We Are

Tau Epsilon Kappa (TEK) at Indiana University is a selective, co-ed professional technology fraternity built for students who want to go beyond the classroom.

What We Do

We host technical workshops, industry panels, and hands-on projects that prepare our members for top careers in tech and business.

Our Community

TEK is a network of ambitious students who collaborate, support one another, and build lifelong professional connections.

From One Idea to a Growing Legacy

Tau Epsilon Kappa at Indiana University began with a vision — to build a community where students passionate about technology and innovation could grow together.

Founded by Neeharika Nair and Alekhya Vadiala, the IU chapter represents the Beta Chapter of Tau Epsilon Kappa.

What started as an idea quickly became a movement — bringing together driven students seeking more than just a resume boost.

Founding Vision

TEK IU was built to empower students, foster collaboration, and create lasting impact on campus and beyond.
